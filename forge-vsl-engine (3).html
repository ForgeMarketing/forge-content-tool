Script Angle Guide
Problem agitation → mechanism
Opens by poking the viewer’s biggest pain point until they feel it viscerally, then positions your offer as the only logical solution. Best for cold audiences who don’t know you yet.
Proof-led credibility
Leads with your single most powerful client result in the first 5 seconds. Builds trust immediately, then connects the result to your process so the viewer understands why it worked and believes it can work for them.
Contrarian reframe
Challenges a widely held belief your audience currently has — something they’ve been told or assumed is true. Creates a “I never thought about it that way” moment that makes them stop and reconsider. Strong pattern interrupt.
Mechanism reveal
Pulls back the curtain on exactly how your system or process works. Educates the viewer so thoroughly that they feel like they’re getting value even before buying. Creates desire through understanding.
Founder / behind the scenes
The spokesperson shares a personal insight, story, or behind-the-scenes perspective about what actually works in their industry. Builds parasocial trust and positions them as a real expert — not a polished actor reading a script.
Diagnostic / qualifier
Opens by calling out a specific symptom the ideal customer is experiencing right now. Makes them feel seen and understood before you’ve said anything about your offer. Best for pre-qualifying leads and filtering for high-intent buyers.
Math / leverage (CPL angle)
Uses a specific number or simple math equation in the first 3 seconds to show how one metric — if improved — changes everything else downstream. Makes the ROI case visceral and undeniable. Works especially well for lead gen and service businesses.
